About Us

Hero Tools was founded in 2020 as a way to show off the Opti B2B Commerce Cloud platform. Hero is designed as a branded manufacturer and has the common features of a branded manufacturer embedded in it. Hero specializes in power tools including nailers, staplers, grinders and rotary hammers.
